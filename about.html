What drives us
Our core values
Every decision we make comes back to these three principles.

Farm-first sourcing

We go directly to farmers — cutting out middlemen so your produce is fresher and farmers are paid fairly. Every supplier is vetted in person.

Radical transparency

Every product shows its farm origin, harvest date, and certifications. No hidden ingredients. No misleading labels. Just honest food.

Community first

A portion of every order supports local food banks and sustainable farming programs in underserved areas across the region.

Zero-waste packaging

100% compostable delivery packaging since 2020. We've eliminated over 4 tonnes of single-use plastic from our supply chain.

Speed without sacrifice

Fast delivery shouldn't mean stale produce. Our cold-chain logistics ensures your groceries stay fresh from harvest to doorstep.

Uncompromising quality

Every batch passes a 7-point quality check before dispatch. If it doesn't meet our standards, it goes back — not to you.

How we got here
Our journey
From a corner stall to your doorstep — a decade of growing together.
2014
The first stall
Thomas and Priya Nair opened a small organic stall at the Riverside Farmers Market with just 12 products and a handwritten price board.
2017
First physical store
Demand outgrew the stall. Stackly opened its first brick-and-mortar store on Green Lane, stocking over 400 organic products.
2020
Online store launch
During the pandemic, we rapidly built stackly.com and introduced same-day delivery — reaching 10,000 customers in the first 3 months.
2023
80+ farm network
Signed direct partnerships with 80 farms across five states, guaranteeing fair trade pricing and zero middlemen in our supply chain.
Today
120,000 households served
Stackly now delivers to over 120,000 households with a 60-minute delivery guarantee and an NPS score of 78.
