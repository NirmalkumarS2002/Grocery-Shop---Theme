Who We Are

We started Stackly because good food shouldn't need a middleman

What began as one van delivering vegetables from a single farm outside Pune has grown into a network of forty-two family farms, still run on the same idea: the fewer hands between soil and plate, the better the food tastes.

How We Got Here

Our story, in four chapters

2019

One van, one farm

Three friends and a single delivery route through a Pune neighbourhood, twice a week.

2021

Twelve farms join

Word spread among growers tired of selling through three layers of middlemen.

2023

Same-day promise

We rebuilt our routes so anything ordered before noon reaches you by evening.

2026

Forty-two farms strong

Now serving five cities, still answering the phone ourselves when something's wrong.

What We Stand For

Three rules we don't bend on

01

Pay farmers fairly, first

Growers are paid before the produce even leaves the farm, not after it sells.

02

Nothing sits in storage

If it isn't sold within a day of picking, it doesn't go out under our name.

03

Tell you where it came from

Every order lists the exact farm it was grown on, no vague sourcing labels.
